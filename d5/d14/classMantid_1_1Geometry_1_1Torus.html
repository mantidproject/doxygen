Detailed Description

Holds a torus in vector form.

Author
S. Ansell
Date
December 2006
Version
1.0

Defines a Torus as a centre, normal and three parameters:

  • Iradius :: inner radius of the torus
  • Dradius :: displaced radius (the radius away from the plane)
  • Displacement :: elipse displacment from the centre. These are c,b,a in that order.
