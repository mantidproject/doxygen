Detailed Description

PolarizationCorrectionWildes : This algorithm corrects for non-ideal component efficiencies in polarized neutron analysis.

It is based on [A. R. Wildes (2006) Neutron News, 17:2, 17-25, DOI: 10.1080/10448630600668738]
