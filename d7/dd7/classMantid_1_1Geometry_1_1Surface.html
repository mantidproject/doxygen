Detailed Description

Holds a basic quadratic surface.

Author
S. Ansell
Date
April 2004
Version
1.0

Holds a basic surface with equation form

\[ Ax^2+By^2+Cz^2+Dxy+Exz+Fyz+Gx+Hy+Jz+K=0 \]
